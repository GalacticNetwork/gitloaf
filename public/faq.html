FAQ

Why do some games from 3kh0 not work?

Because Gitloaf is in a very early beta stage, we do not support some games. We are hoping to fix this issue in the future.


Why does it take so long to load?

Usually, the loading is minimal and takes less than 5 seconds. However, when our server is under high load, it may take longer.


Are you affiliated with github or other CDNs?

No. We are not affiliated with anyone besides THJ and Rblocker.


How can I report a bug or error?

You can report it in the THJ discord, or open an issue on the github.
